ğŸ‘¥ 4 servings

ğŸ›’ Ingredients

â€¢ 1 onion, sliced
â€¢ 100g spinach

â€¢ 4 tbsp soy sauce
â€¢ 2 tbsp sugar
â€¢ 3 tbsp sesame oil
â€¢ Sesame seeds for garnish

ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ³ Instructions

  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve warm or at room temperature.

ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ³ Instructions
